Cheesecake

Description

This creamy cheesecake is great with a variety of toppings -or even by itself!

Ingredients (for 12 servings):

  • 1 1/2 cup(s) graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup(s) margarine
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cup(s) white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, 3 tablespoons of sugar, cinnamon and melted margarine.
    • Mix all ingredients well and press into the bottom and sides of a 9 inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
    • Blend in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla and salt.
    • Fold in the sour cream.
  4. Pour batter into springform pan.
    • Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
  5. Turn off oven and leave the cake to cool off for at least 30 minutes.

Enjoy!
