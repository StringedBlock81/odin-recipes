Sweet and Sour Orange Chicken

Description

With this recipe you'll learn how to make this delicious dish.

You'll learn how to prepare and fry the chicken, so that you get that nice and lovely crunchy texture; how to make the sweet and sour sauce so that your family ends up licking their fingers, and wanting another serving!

Ingredients (for 8 servings):

Chicken:

  • 1 cup(s) ice water
  • 1 egg(s)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cup(s) all-purpose flour, divided
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup(s) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup(s) ketchup
  • 1/2 cup(s) white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup(s) water
  • 1/2 cup(s) orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped fresh pineapple (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons pineapple juice
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. Beat the ice water and egg together in a bowl using a whisk or a fork. Add baking soda and salt and mix it. Add 1 cup of flour to the mixture; mix until blended but lumpy.
  2. Place the remaining cup of flour in a separate bowl. Cover each piece of chicken in flour, then move to egg mixture and soak it, let excess batter drip off.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok, or large deep frying pan, over medium heat until a drop of water crackles when placed into the oil.
  4. Fry the chicken pieces for about 3 to 4 minutes, flipping them if needed, until they no longer have a pink color inside, and acquire a golden-brown crust.
  5. Take them out, remove the oil excess, and keep them warm.
  6. Combine the cornstarch and 3 tablespoons of water in a small bowl, and mix it well.
  7. Put the sugar, ketchup, vinegar, 1/2 cup of water, orange marmalade, pineapple (Optional), pineapple juice, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes into a medium saucepan, heat it until boiling point; add the cornstarch mixture and stir it up.
  8. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring it frequently, until the desired consistency is acquired.
  9. Pour the sauce over the chicken, mix it well and serve.

Enjoy!
