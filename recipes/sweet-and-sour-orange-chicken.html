Sweet and Sour Orange Chicken

Description

With this recipe you'll learn how to make this delicious dish.

You'll learn how to prepare and fry the chicken, so that you get that nice and lovely crunchy texture; how to make the sweet and sour sauce so that your family ends up licking their fingers, and wanting another serving!

Ingredients (for 8 servings):

Chicken:

  • 1 cup(s) ice water
  • 1 egg(s)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cup(s) all-purpose flour, divided
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup(s) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup(s) ketchup
  • 1/2 cup(s) white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup(s) water
  • 1/2 cup(s) orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped fresh pineapple (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons pineapple juice
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
