Spring Vegetable Soup

Description

With this recipe you'll learn how to prepare this tasty dish perfect for when your children or a loved one get sick with the flu.

Just follow along with the instructions and you'l end up with an exquisite soup, comprised of a mouth watering chicken broth and soft vegetables.

Ingredients (for 6 servings):

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 mid sized potato, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped broccoli
  • 1/2 cup(s) frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup(s) torn spinach
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup(s) chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup(s) chopped cabbage
  • 2 (32 fluid oz) containers chicken broth
  • 6 oz egg noodles
  • 1 cup(s) canned white beans

Steps:

  1. Pour the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Throw in the onion and garlic; stir till you can smell the cooked onion.
  2. Stir in the potato, broccoli, corn, spinach, mushrooms, carrots,and cabbage.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 20 minutes, or when the potato is tender.
  5. Add the egg noodles and white beans into the pot; continue cooking for about 7 minutes, or until the noodles are tender and beans are heated through.
